How does it works

Overall process

  1. Company register on our website.
  2. individual register on our website.
  3. Company create advertisement post.
  4. Individual pick advertisement post.
  5. Agreement being done.
  6. Website create and paste sticker on individual's car.
  7. Monthly confirmation and transaction being made.

Company

  • Companies fill in information for creating an advertisement post with the following details:
    • Required type of vehicles that would want to post up their advertisement (Cars, Motorcycles, tricycles, trucks, etc.)
    • Type of sticker available for each type of vehicle (e.g. car window, car door, and truck side).
    • The price company would offered for each kind of sticker (per month).
    • Required number of stickers of each kind.

Individual

  1. Individual create an account with the following details:
    • User's personal information such as name, age, birthdate, id number, driver license, etc.
  2. Vehicle to be added to user porfile with following details:
    • Car license plate number, car registration number, car license expiration date, parts of car available for advertisement, etc.
  3. Pick an advertisement on his/her car.
  4. Enter the company advertisement section of the webpage.
  5. Choose a suitable option of advertisement from a prefered comapany.
  6. Pick a kind of sticker.
  7. Duration of advertisement sticking (per month).
  8. Pick a vehicle to be putting up advertisement from the list.
  9. Website will auto-check the selected option.
  10. If pass, then individual will have to sign an agreement to put up the sticker on the car by the selected period of time.
  11. Come to our website company to pick up the sticker.
