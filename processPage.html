HOW IT WORKS?

  • Step 1

    Company Registration

    Company register and become a member on our website.

  • Step 2

    Car Owner Registration

    Individual register and become member on our website.

  • Step 3

    Create advertisement post

    Companies fill in an online application for creating an advertisement post which would be posted publicly on our website.

  • Step 4

    Individual Select Advertisement Post

    Individual select an advertisement post to be wrapped on his/her car, and fill in an application on how they preferred the advertisement to be posted.

  • Step 5

    Agreement

    Individual will have to sign agree to an agreement on putting up the sticker on his/her car by the selected period of time, will follow all the stated conditions.

  • Step 6

    Sticker Production and Wrap

    I SAW IT will create the specified sticker, and on the selected date the car owner will have to come to the company for getting the sticker pasted on his/her car.

  • Step 7

    Monthly Confirmation and Transaction

    Monthly confirmation: Individual will have to take a photo of QR code on the sticker to confirm that he/she has not taken it off yet. After monthly confirmation, a transaction will be made.
